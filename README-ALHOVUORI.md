# Alhovuori - Adventure Park & Community Village

## Overview

Alhovuori is a 50-hectare property in Lohja, Finland, approximately one hour from Helsinki. The property features unique natural and built assets that present exceptional opportunities for community-driven development and sustainable tourism.

## Property Details

**Location:** Ikkainen, Lohja, Finland
**Total Area:** ~50 hectares
- Forest: ~20 hectares
- Private lake with shoreline
- Mountain slope terrain

## Community Model

### Cooperative Structure
The project envisions a **cooperative ownership model** where:
- Members purchase ownership shares (€10,000-€50,000)
- Democratic decision-making processes
- Shared governance and community rules
- Mix of private spaces and communal areas
- Balance between individual freedom and collective responsibility

### Key Principles
- **Sustainability**: Permaculture, forest management, renewable energy
- **Community**: Collaborative decision-making, shared resources
- **Economic Viability**: Multiple revenue streams (tourism, events, accommodation, restaurant)
- **Preservation**: Respect for natural environment and existing infrastructure
- **Innovation**: New approaches to rural living and recreation

## Development Priorities

### Phase 1: Essential Infrastructure
- Renovate residential building for core community members
- Restore one sauna to full operation
- Assess and repair ski lift (if feasible)
- Basic trail development and signage

### Phase 2: Revenue Generation
- Complete barn-to-restaurant conversion
- Establish camping and accommodation operations
- Develop guided tour and activity programs
- Launch summer camp programs

### Phase 3: Expansion
- Build additional cabins or tiny homes
- Develop advanced trail systems
- Create event hosting infrastructure
- Establish permaculture demonstration sites

### Phase 4: Long-term Vision
- Full adventure park with multiple activities
- Year-round operation capabilities
- Educational center and workshop spaces
- Sustainable community living model

## Economic Framework

### Investment Opportunities
- **Ownership Shares**: €10,000 to €50,000 per member
- **Cabin Plot Rights**: Additional investment for private building plots
- **Sweat Equity**: Contribution of time and skills valued toward membership

### Revenue Streams
- Summer camp bookings
- Restaurant and catering operations
- Event venue rental
- Accommodation (camping, cabins, rooms)
- Guided activities and outdoor education
- Forest products and sustainable harvesting
- Membership fees and facility usage

### Financial Sustainability
- Diverse income sources reduce risk
- Seasonal operations balanced with year-round activities
- Community member contributions reduce labor costs
- Shared resources minimize individual expenses
